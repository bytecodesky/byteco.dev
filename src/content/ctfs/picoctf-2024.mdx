## Overview

picoCTF 2024 was an exciting competition that ran from March 12-26, 2024. As one of the largest and most accessible CTF competitions, it attracted thousands of participants worldwide.

## Competition Format

- **Duration**: 2 weeks
- **Team Size**: Individual or teams
- **Categories**: Web Exploitation, Cryptography, Reverse Engineering, Binary Exploitation, Forensics, and General Skills

## Statistics

- Over 10,000 participants
- 100+ challenges across 6 categories
- Points ranged from 50 to 500

## Highlights

This competition was particularly well-suited for beginners while still offering challenging problems for experienced players. The challenges progressively increased in difficulty, providing an excellent learning experience.
